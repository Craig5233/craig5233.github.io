The First Network Hack

A generation ago, while the internet was in it's infancey, a little know hacker named John Draper who went by the pseudonym Captain Crunch gained access to the AT&T phone network by using a toy whistle from Cap'n Crunch cereal, which enabled him to make long distance phone calls, This was known as phone "Phreaking".

The First Internet "Hack"

I personally wouldn't call it a hack, however, it was a "Denial of Service" attack that was caused by a worm produced by Robert Morris in 1989.
